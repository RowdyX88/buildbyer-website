Our Heritage

Founded in 1984 by Elizabeth Hati, Her Majesty's Hati began as a boutique firm specializing in corporate law during the golden age of Wall Street.

What began as a three-person operation in a Manhattan brownstone has grown into one of the most respected legal practices worldwide, while maintaining our commitment to personalized service.

Our name reflects both our founder's regal approach to the law and her Indonesian heritage ("Hati" meaning "heart" in Bahasa), representing the compassion at our core.
